--- Armor Buffs ---
by GabeHasWon
commissioned by HeavyDutyDerby

This mod adds in a TON of minor edits to make most armors in prehardmode feel more interesting, alongside some vanities.

GitHub: https://github.com/GabeHasWon/ArmorBuffs

---

All changes are to set bonuses, unless specified:

Copper armor: 
+3 flat damage

Tin armor: 
+5 armour penetration

Iron armor: 
15% increased melee size

Lead armor: 
Knockback immunity, 10% slower movement speed

Silver armor: 
+1 flat health regeneration/second

Tungsten armor: 
+33% weapon knockback

Gold armor: 
50% for all drops to drop twice

Ashwood Pants (single item): 
All attacks inflict "On Fire" for 6 seconds

(Pink) Snow Armor: 
50% chance for all attacks to inflict "Frostburn" for 3 seconds

Archaeologist's vanity set: 
Permanent dangersense & hunter potion effects, no longer vanity

Ninja and Crystal Assassin armor: 
+10% chance to dodge any attacks

Viking Helmet (single item): 
4 -> 12 defense, 10% slower movement speed

Necro armor: 
+35% bullet velocity, and after death, survive an additional seven seconds, then die

Wizard Hat (single item): 
5% -> 15% magic damage

Magic hat (single item): 
+10% magic crit chance

Diamond Robe (single item): 
+15% magic crit chance, max mana 80 -> 100, requires 70 bones to craft

Rune Hat (single item): 
+7 defense, +15% increased magic damage (set bonus with Rune Robe/ no pants needed: +25% magic crit chance), no longer vanity

Rune Robe (single item): 
+4 defence, increase maximum mana by 120, 20% reduced mana cost, no longer vanity

Rune Hat + Rune Robe set bonus: 
+25% magic crit chance

Royal Gel (single item): 
Any attack has a 15% chance to inflict the "Oiled" debuff for 4 seconds

Oiled now works on players as well.
